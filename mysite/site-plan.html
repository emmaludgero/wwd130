Overview

Purpose

Imagine you just finished your fantastic trip! You made a lot of remarkable memories. But you forgot to buy your souvenir, or it was too expensive, and you didn't. What can you do about it? Imagine if you could buy it online at a low price and personalized! My Only One Souvenir exists for that reason. We want to make your memories to be remarkable forever. Keep up your fantastic trips and be happy buying My Only One Souvenir.

Content

Who We Are

We are a society of two sisters: sister one, Emma Katherine, the nerd, and sister two, Maria Victoria, the idea maker. Maria Victoria graduated in Marketing from Estácio College. She is a Sales Bank. She lives in Curitiba, loves to travel, is very organized, and thrives. Emma Katherine is a student of Applied Technology from Brigham Young University. She lives in Curitiba, loves to design and create, and is fighting for her success. We want to travel the world and have something to remember about that marvelous adventure. But sometimes, that's not possible. All we have is pictures and the memory. We don't know what to do with those pictures and forget about them. And our memory is flawed, so we forget the good times. So, we created this site so that we can provide more extended travel moments in our lives.

How Our Logistic Works

We are a Brazilian company. Our factory is located in Paranaguá, in the Paraná State. We use the Post Office for local orders and shipping to international orders, with a difference of a few more days. We have partnerships with FedEx, TNT, DHL, and Panalpina. Your souvenir will get home safely.
